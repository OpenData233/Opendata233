About

Opendata233 provides Ghanaians with the necessary information to help them vote based on issues.


Mission

Our mission is to use the continuous series of survey data to help us investigate electoral behavior and attitudes in Ghana.

Our focus includes voter perceptions of the major political parties, the candidates, national and international issues, and of the importance of the election.

Also we aim to explore voter expectations about the outcome of the election, degree of voter interest in politics, political affiliation and voting history, as well as participation in the electoral process.
